Penguin

While other birds have wings for flying, penguins have adapted flippers to help them swim in the water.

  • Kingdom:Animalia
  • Family: Spheniscidae
  • Genus: Acinonyx
  • Class:Aves

Penguins: a group of aquatic flightless birds with flippers instead of wings that live almost exclusively in the Southern Hemisphere.Their height ranges from 15 inches to 3.5 feet. Their weight ranges from 2 pounds to 88 pounds. Emperor penguins are the largest species, with an average height of 45 inches tall. Emperor penguins can weigh up to 88 pounds.
